challenge

After holding a significant share of the Asian
frozen food market for decades, Ling Ling was
confronted by a sudden influx of competition for
coveted shelf space. The Japanese-owned company
was confident in the quality and authenticity of their
products, and challenged us to highlight that
advantage.

Approach

We proposed “Pass it down” as a campaign that
could speak to Ling Ling’s authentic Asian heritage in an
inspirational way while also serving as a conversational
call to action around the dinner table.

02/
CAMPAIGN
05/

PASS IT DOWN
CAMPAIGN LOCKUP

Utilizing the brands’ ink drawn “L” from their wordmark as the downward arrow in the lockup created a sense of motion and direction and allowed us to use this as a brand element in the designs. With the authenticity of their ancient dishes at the forefront of the campaign, we took inspiration from traditional Japanese ink paintings and used that as texture within the tagline and headlines.

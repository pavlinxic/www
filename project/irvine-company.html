challenge

The Irvine Company team was looking to
create a seamless digital experience that simplified
their residents' daily lives by enabling them to easily
manage their home, interact with the community, and
empower the Irvine lifestyle.

Approach

Our strategy for redesigning the All at Home
app focused on blending intuitive design for a wide
range of residents with creating an extension of the
Irvine Company brand. This allowed us to deliver an
experience that enhances the daily lives of residents.

02/
ALL AT HOME APP
05/

THE ALL AT HOME
EXPERIENCE

All at Home is an essential tool for Irvine Company apartment residents. With its user-friendly design and comprehensive features, the app enhances convenience and community engagement, transforming the apartment living experience.

A POWERFUL TOOL FOR EVERYDAY LIVING

The new design system provides residents with easily accessible features, enabling them to manage their home and interact with their community.

A FLEXIBLE SYSTEM
FOR ALL DEVICES

While the majority of residence use the mobile app, the All at Home experience was built for all form factors. The flexible design system enabled us to build a seamless transition on the web from mobile to desktop.
